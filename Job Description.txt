**Job Description: AI/ML Engineer (Fresher)**


**Position Title:** AI/ML Engineer (Entry-Level)  
**Location:** [City/Remote]  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time  
**Reports To:** AI/ML Team Lead or Data Science Manager  


**Job Overview:**  
We are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic AI/ML Engineer (Fresher) to join our team. As an entry-level AI/ML Engineer, you will work on developing, testing, and deploying machine learning models and AI solutions under the guidance of senior engineers. This role is ideal for recent graduates or individuals with foundational knowledge in AI, machine learning, and data science, eager to kickstart their career in a fast-evolving tech field.


**Key Responsibilities:**  
- Assist in designing, developing, and implementing machine learning models and algorithms to solve business problems.  
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand project requirements and translate them into technical solutions.  
- Perform data preprocessing, cleaning, and analysis to prepare datasets for model training.  
- Train, test, and validate machine learning models using frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn.  
- Support senior engineers in optimizing and fine-tuning ML models for performance and scalability.  
- Contribute to the development of AI-driven applications, including natural language processing (NLP), computer vision, or predictive analytics.  
- Document processes, model performance, and project outcomes for internal reference and knowledge sharing.  
- Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI/ML technologies and tools.  
- Participate in code reviews, team meetings, and brainstorming sessions to enhance project outcomes.  


**Required Skills and Qualifications:**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related field (Master’s degree is a plus).  
- Strong understanding of machine learning concepts (e.g., supervised/unsupervised learning, regression, classification, clustering).  
- Proficiency in programming languages such as **Python** or **R** (Python preferred).  
- Familiarity with ML frameworks and libraries like **TensorFlow**, **PyTorch**, **Scikit-learn**, or **Keras**.  
- Basic knowledge of data structures, algorithms, and software development principles.  
- Experience with data manipulation tools like **Pandas**, **NumPy**, or similar.  
- Understanding of basic statistical concepts and data analysis techniques.  
- Familiarity with version control systems like **Git** is a plus.  
- Strong problem-solving skills and eagerness to learn new technologies.  
- Good communication skills and ability to work in a collaborative team environment.  


**Preferred Skills (Optional):**  
- Exposure to cloud platforms like **AWS**, **Google Cloud**, or **Azure** for ML model deployment.  
- Basic knowledge of deep learning concepts (e.g., neural networks, CNNs, RNNs).  
- Experience with data visualization tools like **Matplotlib**, **Seaborn**, or **Tableau**.  
- Hands-on experience from internships, academic projects, or open-source contributions in AI/ML.  


**What We Offer:**  
- Opportunity to work on cutting-edge AI/ML projects with real-world impact.  
- Mentorship from experienced AI/ML professionals to guide your career growth.  
- A collaborative and innovative work environment.  
- Access to learning resources and training programs to enhance your skills.  
- Competitive salary and benefits package (specifics may vary by company).
